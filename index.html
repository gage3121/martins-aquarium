Care Tips

Blobfish-

Tank Cleaning:

Per the conditions of the tank no cleaning is necessary because things like vegetation simply can't survive.

Water Salinity:

35 PPT or 35 parts salt for every 1,000 parts water.

Water Temperature:

Slightly above freezing at 36° F

Lionfish-

Tank Cleaning:

Be very careful as the lionfish has venomous spines but clean the tank regularly.

Water Salinity:

35PPT

Water Temperature:

They prefer a water temperature of around 17.5° C or 63.5° F.

Moon Jellyfish-

Tank Cleaning:

Jellyfish are sensitive to rapid changes in salinity, temperature, pH, etc. Therefor their tanks must be cleaned and maintained regularly in order to keep them safe and happy.

Water Salinity:

35PPT

Water Temperature:

Moon Jellyfish need a water temperature of about 19° C or 66° F.
